# Principle 1

## 🧮 Engines own the truth.

```text
Business Engine

↓

Verified Facts
```

The engine is responsible for:

* Reorder Quantity
* Revenue Risk
* Profit Risk
* Dead Stock
* Overstock
* Business Health
* Transfer Suggestions
* Forecast Numbers

No AI.

Pure deterministic logic.

---

# Principle 2

## 🤖 AI owns the conversation.

AI is responsible for:

* Explaining
* Summarizing
* Prioritizing
* Teaching
* Answering Questions
* Writing Reports
* Executive Communication

Never calculations.

---

# Principle 3

## 📊 Never fabricate business data.

If NOVA knows it

✅ Show it.

If NOVA predicts it

⚠️ Label it **Forecast**.

If NOVA doesn't know

Don't invent it.

---

# Principle 5

## 🎯 Every AI answer must be traceable.

Imagine the CEO asks:

> Why?

NOVA should always answer

```
Because...

Current Stock = 84

Daily Sales = 42

Lead Time = 5

Engine Recommendation = REORDER

Estimated Revenue Risk = ₹24,000
```

Every AI answer should be backed by evidence.

---

# Principle 6

## 🧠 AI never overrides an engine.

This is huge.

AI can say

> Here's why the engine recommends reordering.

AI can NEVER say

> Ignore the engine.

The engine wins.

Always.

# Principle 8

## 👔 Think like a COO, not a chatbot.

A chatbot answers questions.

A COO answers:

* What should I do today?
* Why?
* What happens if I wait?
* What changed?
* What is my biggest risk?
* Where am I losing money?
* Which decision has the biggest ROI?

That's the mindset NOVA should have.

---

# Principle 9

## 🏢 Business-first, AI-second.

People shouldn't buy NOVA because it has AI.

They should buy it because it improves inventory decisions.

The AI makes those decisions easier to understand and act on.

---
